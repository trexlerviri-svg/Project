Our Newest Show

The Brothers Lionheart

Skorpan is a young and fearful boy who knows he will die soon. His brother, Jonathan, tells him of the land of Nangijala to where one journeys in the afterlife. It’s a place of campfires and true adventure, the place from where all adventures originate. But there are adventures that should never be visited. The two boys unite in Nangijala as the Brothers Lionheart where they fight for freedom in the Rose Valley, one of Nangijala’s two valleys occupied by the evil Tengil. The Brothers Lionheart is a timeless tale about death and rebirth, love and loyalty, and the moral necessity of standing against tyranny. It invites children — and adults — to take the characters by the hand and journey into a world where imagination becomes a refuge, and where even the smallest, most frightened heart can change the course of history. Take the children by the hand and journey with them to Astrid Lindgren’s magical land of Nangijala.

Director: Thomas Hendrixen

Dramatizator: Line Morkeby

Music Composer:Jonas West

Main Actors:Jonathan Bergholdt,Alvin Olid,Mette Maria,Martin Dupont

Family Friendly

Fun for all ages

Share laughter, thrills, and truly unforgettable moments with your children, grandchildren, or loved ones this winter. Step into a world of adventure and imagination, where courage is tested, friendships are forged, and magic unfolds live on stage. From the very first scene, audiences of all ages will be swept into an enchanting journey filled with wonder, excitement, and heartfelt emotions. Follow the brave Lionheart brothers as they face challenges, discover their inner strength, and learn the true meaning of loyalty and love. Watch the joy on your little ones’ faces as their eyes light up with every daring escape, magical moment, and triumphant victory — and feel your own heart warm as you experience the story together. Perfect for families of all generations, this heartwarming play invites everyone to laugh, cheer, and be inspired. More than just a performance, it’s a shared experience that brings people closer, sparks imagination, and creates precious memories that will be cherished long after the curtains close. A truly magical outing for the winter holidays — one your family will remember for years to come.
